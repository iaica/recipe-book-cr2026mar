# Salmorejo

## Ingredients

- 1 kg ripe tomatoes
- 200 g stale bread (preferably from a rustic loaf)
- 100 ml extra virgin olive oil
- 1 garlic clove
- Salt to taste
- Optional toppings: chopped boiled egg, diced jamón serrano, a drizzle of olive oil

## Instructions

- Wash and chop the tomatoes. Blend them until completely smooth.
- Strain the blended tomatoes through a sieve to remove seeds and skin.
- Add the bread to the tomato purée and let it soak for a few minutes.
- Add the garlic clove and a pinch of salt. Blend again until smooth.
- With the blender running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil to emulsify the mixture.
- Chill in the fridge for at least 1 hour before serving.
- Serve cold and top with chopped boiled egg, jamón serrano, and a drizzle of olive oil.
